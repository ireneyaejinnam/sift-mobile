03 · Why now

The conditions for a taste graph just landed.

Three shifts converge in 2026. None of them existed five years ago.

AI curation got cheap

An LLM rubric that scores 1,000 events for audience fit costs <$5/wk. The same job needed a five-person editorial team in 2020.

Taste graphs went mainstream

Beli, TikTok, and Spotify trained a generation to swipe-rate-share. Users now expect recs to learn from them — events haven't caught up.

Aggregators are stale

Eventbrite hasn't shipped a meaningful UX change in 5 years. Editorial sites still publish by hand. None personalize. The window is open now.
