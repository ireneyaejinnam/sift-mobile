08 · How the taste graph works

Curate hard. Score every signal. Re-rank in real time. The model compounds.

Step 1 · Curate

Reject 85% before it touches a user.

  • AI ingest from 6 tastemaker sources (Ticketmaster, Eater, Infatuation, TimeOut, Resy, Dice)
  • LLM rubric scores audience fit, venue quality, verified dates, real prices
  • Human editor reviews keep-pile weekly — no fabricated listings
  • Eventbrite structurally can't do this — their model needs the firehose.
Step 2 · Signal

Three signals, three weights.

  • Swipe — soft signal. Right boosts category, left dampens.
  • Feedback sheet — explicit "more / less like this" retunes a whole vibe.
  • Save / Going — beacon. Similar events climb the deck immediately.
  • No nightly batch. No cold-start wait. Re-rank in <200ms.
Step 3 · Compound

The model is the moat.

  • ~20 swipes warms a profile — one session.
  • Every swipe across every user makes the next swipe smarter.
  • City-agnostic: same rubric, swap the editorial lead, repeat.
  • By month 6 the taste graph is the asset — not the catalog.

15 · Risks & mitigations

What could break this — and exactly what we do about it.

Risk 01 · Distribution

Cold-start beyond friends-of-friends.

Every event app that's tried social has died bootstrapping the network. Paid acquisition without payback kills runway.

Mitigation

Creator + curator seeding, not paid social.

Eater and Infatuation curate inside the app — their audience is already ours. Referral unlocks lists. Paid social only after LTV/CAC > 3 on validated creator content.

Risk 02 · Catalog scaling

Hand-curation doesn't scale to 5 cities.

The 85% reject rate depends on a human reviewer. Hiring 5 editorial leads is slow and expensive.

Mitigation

One editorial lead per city — same playbook.

The AI rubric does 90% of the cut; the human reviews the keep-pile (~30 min/wk). One lead per city is the model. City-agnostic rubric is the moat.

Risk 03 · Incumbent response

Eventbrite or TikTok ships a "for you" feed.

If a giant copies the loop, our window closes.

Mitigation

Their model can't reject the firehose.

Eventbrite's economics need 100% inventory live. TikTok ranks creators, not events. The taste graph compounds — by month 6 the model is the moat, not the catalog.
